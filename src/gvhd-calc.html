What is Graft-Versus-Host Disease?

Graft-Versus-Host Disease (GVHD) may occur when the new stem cells (graft) recognize the tissues of your body (host) as foreign and starts a reaction. GVHD may cause a fever and commonly affects the skin, gastrointestinal tract (mouth, esophagus, stomach, and intestines) and liver.
